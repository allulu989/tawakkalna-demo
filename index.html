About Me

I'm an Information Technology graduate who found her passion at the intersection of people, products, and problem-solving. What draws me to Product Management is the challenge of truly understanding why users behave the way they do — and turning those insights into features that genuinely make their lives easier.

At SDAIA, I've shaped features across Tawakkalna and Wakeb — from designing a new identity layer that balances expression with privacy, to studying secure chat transfer solutions, to building analytics plans that track what actually matters. With every feature, I ask two questions: Does this solve a real problem? and Will users actually understand and love it?

I care deeply about clarity — in requirements, in user flows, and in how teams communicate. I believe the best products are built in the small details, through honest iteration and a willingness to question assumptions.

User-Centric

I start with the user — their needs, pains, and moments of friction.

Data-Informed

I believe decisions get stronger when paired with the right metrics.

Detail-Obsessed

Great products live in the edge cases, user flows, and small touches.
